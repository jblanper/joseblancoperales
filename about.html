My name is José Blanco Perales and I am an art historian with a great interest in the artistics movements classified under the term New Media Art (generative art, net art, demoscene, livecoding, algorithmic art, vjing...). In the last years I taught myself how to code python and javascript, and I started to programm some web apps to produce designs inspired by the artists of the new media movement. I find really interesting the use of algorithms for producing art and I love the digital medium for its openess and capacity of difussion.

In this webpage I post in the Gallery section some of the still images I produced programming in javascript in the browser. The interactive projects and animations are linked in the Projects section of this page.
